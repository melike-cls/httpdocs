What is the All on Four technique? What is All on 4 Implant?

The All on Four technique is a safe implant technique that has been applied for 20 years and promises scientifically successful results. It is a healthy method applied for patients who have lost their teeth due to various reasons, especially aged patients, and who have osteoporosis. Thanks to the All on Four technique, a total of four implants, two each in the back and front of the jaw, are used without the need for a separate implant procedure for each tooth. and dental prosthesis is placed on these implants. In the standard implant technique, which can take up to one year, advanced surgical interventions may be required and the cost is high. During this period, the patient may remain toothless for a long time. The All on Four implant technique, on the other hand, can be applied quickly by placing the temporary fixed dental prosthesis in one day, with a single surgical intervention, and the patient is fitted with permanent fixed teeth within two to three months. In this way, patients do not remain toothless during the treatment. The All on 4 implant application has a lower cost for patients compared to the standard implant technique.

How is All on Four Implant Dental Treatment performed?

  • All on Four implant technique is applied to the patient after medical imaging examinations such as x-ray and computed tomography are performed by a specialist dentist and measurement is taken.
  • Temporary prostheses are completed in the laboratory in accordance with the patient's measurements.
  • During the All on Four implant treatment, the patient is given local anesthesia. Stress, excitement, fear, etc. that the patient will experience. In cases, before local anesthesia, the patient is relieved by giving Nitrous oxide, known as laughing gas, this gas is a non-hazardous gas with no side effects and can be used even in children.
  • If the patient has teeth that require intervention (caries, loosening, etc.), implants are placed in his jaw after extraction.
  • The patient's bridges and crowns are fixed to these placed implants.
  • Three to four months are waited for a total of four dental implants placed in the patient's jaw to fuse with the jaw and fully heal.
  • After the recovery period of 3 to 4 months, the necessary controls are made and permanent dental prostheses are placed and the treatment is completed.

What should be considered after All on Four Implant Treatment?

  • Bloody saliva may be encountered within 24 hours after the treatment. At this stage, care should be taken not to rinse the mouth or spit.
  • Do not eat or drink anything for 2 hours after All on Four implant treatment.
  • Bleeding in the form of leakage may occur within the first 24 hours after the treatment, in this case, tamponade is recommended.
  • In the first 24 hours after the treatment, jaw movements should be limited and talking should be as little as possible.
  • Teeth should not be brushed for 36 hours after All on 4 implant treatment.
  • 36 hours after the All on Four implant procedure, mouthwash should be used twice a day on average, and nothing should be consumed until at least 2 hours after the mouthwash.
  • The neck and head should be raised by placing a pillow under the head against swelling and bruising that may occur after the procedure.
  • Smoking and alcohol should not be consumed within the first 24 hours after the treatment. Since smoking will seriously slow down the healing process, it is recommended not to use until the end of the treatment.
  • If swelling occurs in the treated area, ice therapy should be applied.
  • Cold-hot drinks and foods should be avoided due to sensitivities that may occur after the All on Four implant procedure.
  • Patients undergoing treatment should carefully follow the prescription written by the specialist dentist and should not disrupt their controls.
  • Patients should take care not to receive any blows to their faces until they complete the healing process.
  • Patients undergoing All on Four implant treatment should avoid all hard foods during the temporary prosthetic tooth period of 3 or 4 months in order not to damage the placed dental implants. During this process, patients who follow a soft food diet can easily return to their normal eating habits after permanent dentures are placed.
  • Patients should contact our specialist dentists if they have any problems with All on Four implant treatment or if they want to consult.

What is the All on Six technique? What is All on 6 Implant?

The All on Six treatment is a procedure performed with six titanium implants. Fixed dental prosthesis is then placed on six implants, which are fixed to the lower and upper jaw bones, thereby supporting all the teeth in the upper and lower jaws, and placed in harmony with the gingiva. All on 6 treatment is a dental implant treatment that can be safely applied to people with damaged jaw bones or osteoporosis. The All on Six dental implant, which restores the aesthetic appearance and functionality of their teeth to patients who have lost their teeth completely or partially due to different reasons, is considered a long-lasting and healthy implant technique. Patients who have a natural tooth appearance as a result of the All on 6 treatment leave the chewing difficulty problem behind with this treatment.

Who needs All on Six Dental Implants?

All on Six dental implant is a treatment that is applied in cases of edentulism due to different reasons and offers long-lasting solutions. Missing teeth in the lower and upper jaw, posterior or anterior tooth groups are completed with the All on 6 dental implant process. It can also be applied to patients who want to renew their existing teeth in terms of aesthetics and durability. The All on Six prosthesis is much more robust and stable than other prostheses.

What are the advantages of All on Six Dental Implants?

  • The All on Six procedure is completed in a much shorter time than standard implant procedures.
  • It can be applied even to patients who do not have sufficient bone volume for the application of All on 4 implants or whose jawbone is deformed, and may rarely require bone grafting.
  • Provides natural, solid and aesthetically pleasing teeth.
  • It gives patients a younger and more dynamic appearance.
  • recovery time is much shorter compared to other treatment methods.
  • The patient is not toothless at any stage from the beginning to the end of the All on 6 treatment.
  • The temporary prosthesis attached to the patient is replaced when the fixed prosthesis is ready and placed in a single session.
  • After All on Six dental implant treatment, the patient can easily perform eating, drinking, biting and chewing actions by regaining the functionality of the teeth.
  • All on 6 is very long lasting and robust, it is a permanent solution that does not require re-intervention.
  • All on Six dental implant treatment does not require advanced surgical procedures that are used in other implant treatments..
  • It is easy to clean and maintain.
  • It eliminates the speech disorder that develops due to tooth deficiencies.
  • In All on 6 treatment, a solution is offered in a single session and with a single surgical procedure. Implants are placed in the lower and upper jaw by determining the depth, angle and distance. Your jaw is restructured and gains stability.

What are the stages of the All on Six Dental Implant procedure in Istanbul? How is it done?

  • Preparation for the All on Six Dental Implant procedure

    • Before starting the All on Six implant treatment, a preliminary examination is made by our specialist dentists. The examination is completed using medical imaging methods and a precise mouth measurement is taken for the prosthesis to be specially prepared for the patient. The medications used by the patient, current ailments and conditions such as alcohol and cigarette use are reviewed in detail before the treatment. In the All on 6 dental implant procedure, six dental implants are placed in the upper and lower jaw at points determined with precision in terms of angle and support. It is very important that the implants placed receive support from the jawbone. The All on Six application does not require bone grafting. However, if deformation is detected in the jawbone after bone volume scans, it is possible to apply the treatment using the bone graft technique.

  • 3D Modeling in All on Six Dental Treatment

    • Before starting the All on Six implant treatment, our specialist dentists make 3D modeling in the computer environment, and the patient's measurements are taken and sent to the laboratory for the fixed prosthesis to be prepared.

  • Implantation in All on 6 Dental Treatment

    • All on 6 dental implant procedure is performed by giving local anesthesia to the patient. The patient does not feel any pain during the procedure. Nitrous Oxide, which is called laughing gas, is given to relieve the patient before this procedure in reactions such as extreme stress, fear and anxiety. The All on Six implant process takes approximately 2 to 3 hours for each jaw. All procedures required by the treatment can be performed on the same day. Six dental implants are placed at an angle of 90 degrees to the anterior region and 45 degrees to the posterior region.

  • Prosthesis Placement in All on 6 Treatment

    • Immediately after all these procedures, the temporary prosthesis prepared for the patient is placed. By attaching a temporary prosthesis, it is expected that the implants placed will fuse with the bone and the patient will recover, so that patients are not toothless at any stage of the treatment. After a recovery period of about 3-4 months, the necessary controls are made and the permanent prosthesis is placed in a single session.

How long is the recovery time after All on Six Dental Implant Treatment?

After All on Six dental implant treatment, the patient may experience mild pain or discomfort. This problem is eliminated by the antibiotics and painkillers given by the specialist dentist in accordance with the patient's treatment and complaints. During the recovery period, oral care should be performed using the mouthwash recommended by the dentist and attention should be paid to eating and drinking habits.
