Invisalign Treatment

What is Invisalign (Clear Aligners) Treatment?

There are new developments in the field of orthodontics and the number of methods to be applied is increasing day by day. Among these increasing methods, the most used and popular ones are braces and Invisalign treatments. Both treatment methods have their own pros and cons. clear aligners is a type of orthodontic treatment and is one of the newest methods developed in this field. Orthodontic treatment is a treatment that is applied without the need for braces by making plaques designed for the patient. Transparent dental plaque (Invisalign) treatment is an ideal option for patients who have aesthetic concerns due to the appearance of their teeth and do not want to wear braces on their teeth. Transparent plaques are more useful than other traditional methods, both aesthetically and in terms of oral cleaning. One of the most important advantages of the clear alignersis that it corrects the deformity of the teeth before the treatment is understood. Traditional braces treatment is the preferred method for correcting larger problems in tooth structure. Due to the wires and brackets placed on the teeth, it requires more effort because it is cleaned with special apparatus rather than normal oral cleaning. For this reason, recently, Clear Aligners (Invisalign) treatment is more preferred. Orthodontic treatment with transparent aligners is applied to people of all age groups, including children, teenagers and adults. Whether clear aligner treatment can be applied to you will be determined after a detailed examination by a dentist who is an expert in orthodontics. With this treatment method, mostly moderate dental disorders can be eliminated, and traditional orthodontic treatment should be applied for advanced disorders.

How is Invisalign Treatment applied?

After a detailed examination to be performed by a specialist dentist, the size of your upper and lower teeth will be taken and then photos will be taken from inside and outside the mouth. In that day, intraoral x -rays are also taken. The dimensions taken will be transferred to the computer environment and 3D scanning will be given gradually. Transparent plaques are prepared according to the location that the teeth should come and move approximately each plaque teeth 0.5-1.0 mm. If your doctor deems necessary according to the movements of the teeth, he will prepare a treatment plan again. The preparation process of plaques is one or two weeks. In connection with this, clear aligner can be changed to 15 days or weekly depending on the patient's condition. After a while after the plaques are attached to the tooth, the teeth may cause some pain, but it will not last long. Although there is no serious pain, mild painkillers can be used if the patient is forced. Regular changes in the plaques will bring the teeth to the desired position in each change, and when the last plaque is attached, the teeth will now be where they should be, and finally the treatment will be ended by attaching an invisible wire called retainer wire.

Who can be applied to Invisalign Treatment?

Clear aligner treatment has been the most preferred treatment method recently due to its ease of application to patients of all ages, including children, young people and adults. İnvisible aligners is applied to the majority of patients who do not have any problems with the skeletal structure, have a small amount of collapse of their teeth, and in general problems in the tooth structures. When applying this treatment method, it is 10 or 12 according to the age limit, bone structure and collapse of the teeth, but there is no restriction on the upper age and even transparent plaque treatment is preferred by adults due to its aesthetic appearance. Although the age limit is 10 in the clear aligner treatment, your doctor will determine whether your doctor will perform after a detailed examination. There may be situations such as the absence of full development of the jawbone, in these cases, a transparent tooth plaque is not recommended. In short, this procedure is applied to patients of all ages whose problems in their teeth have not progressed. In general, the patients with transparent plaque treatment are as follows; Patients with aesthetic anxiety, patients with low collapse in the teeth, patients who do not have teeth that cannot surface in the jaw bones, those with normal jaw structure and special days (weddings, engagement, graduation, etc.) can be applied to people who care about the appearance.

What are the advantages of Invisalign Treatment in Turkey?

The advantages of the clear aligners (Invisalign treatment) can be listed as follows; Thanks to their removable structures, you can easily remove it at any time during the day and you can easily turn it back at any time. Invisalign therapy gives a good appearance as it does not have a wire appearance as in aesthetically traditional orthodontics. There is no restriction on eating, everything can easily be eaten. In traditional wire treatment, hard foods and extreme hot and excessive cold foods cannot be consumed, but there is no such limitation in this treatment method. Since it does not adversely affect speaking and appearance, it does not cause any problems in terms of self -confidence whereas in the traditional braces method, the patient creates a problem of self -confidence because he has problems while talking while laughing. Since it is simple to remove transparent plaques, oral cleansing can be made as easy and more detailed as in normal times. Since the plaques are frequently changed, the teeth improve more quickly. In the use of transparent dental plaque, oral hygiene can be done in detail and easily. Since traditional braces pressure on teeth and the materials used in treatment cause irritation on lips and cheeks, it causes unrest both at the point of eating and drinking. Clear Aligners have no such cases because they have a smooth and thin structure and are better than other treatments in terms of comfort. Another advantage of these tooth plaques is that it can be applied to people of all ages. It is a great method for patients who think that their age is late for orthodontic treatment and has aesthetic concerns in age. Invisalign (transparent record) treatment allows you to continue your daily activities without any problems, as there is no treatment that will adversely affect your daily life. Above all, metal is not used in this treatment, so there will be no cases such as wounds or irritation in the mouth. The examination time is shorter than traditional wire treatment. One of the important advantages is that transparent plaques are not felt as a treatment process due to ease of use.

Frequently Asked Questions about Invisalign-Clear Aligners Treatment

Can I start Clear Aligners Treatment immediately?

Before starting clear plaque treatment, some procedures should be done. These are primarily a detailed examination and if there are problems that may prevent treatment and to eliminate these problems (such as tartar and caries) on the teeth. The treatment process can be started immediately after.

Is Clear Aligners Treatment painful?

Orthodontic treatments are usually painful, but clear aligners treatment is less painful than traditional methods. A certain pressure should be made in order to move the teeth during the treatment applied to the teeth. This pressure to be made will cause a little bit of pain, but in a short time, the teeth will not be felt in pain because they will get used to this condition.

Can Clear Aligners Treatment be applied to all ages?

Clear aligners treatment can be applied to patients from all age groups. There is no problem in the application of transparent plaque treatment to children. In children, all permanent teeth can be administered after the emergence. This treatment method can be applied to all patients with oral and dental health in other age groups. It is useful to move with your doctor while using your plaques regularly.
