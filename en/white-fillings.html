White Filling - Aesthetic Filling - Composite Filling

What is Aesthetic Filling?

Aesthetic fillers are one of the filling types that provide a very natural appearance and are adjusted according to the patient's tooth color. It is also known as composite filling. Since the aesthetic filling is made of materials that are adjusted according to the patient's tooth color, it is very difficult to distinguish it from natural teeth. Thanks to the developing technology, aesthetic filling procedures in both anterior and posterior teeth can be performed quite simply. Composite filling is quick and simple. Since the filling lines are blurred, its color matches perfectly with the teeth. During this process, grooves are formed in the tooth enamel and a natural appearance is obtained on the upper surface of the tooth. In addition, the teeth retain their functionality. Amalgam fillings (also known as silver fillings) used in the treatment of dental caries do not meet aesthetic expectations. Therefore, porcelain filling and composite filling are more preferred now. Because composite fillings are chemically bonded to the tooth and cured using halogen light, it can also be called beam filling. Amalgam fillings of the patients can be replaced with aesthetic fillings in one or two sessions, depending on the number. Patients who do not want to use fillers due to aesthetic concerns can also easily benefit from this procedure. Since it is very difficult to distinguish with the naked eye from natural teeth in terms of shape and color, it eliminates these aesthetic concerns of the patients. As a result, it provides an extremely natural, bright and aesthetic smile. It also satisfies patients both visually and functionally.

In which situations is Aesthetic Filling required?

Aesthetic filling procedures may be required as a result of some situations such as:

  • When patients want to have an aesthetically beautiful and natural smile, to design the smile of the person.
  • To close the gaps between the teeth that are aesthetically disturbing in some people
  • In cases where permanent discoloration of the teeth occurs
  • When the remaining space after the cleaning of dental caries, which may cause more serious problems, should be filled with filling.
  • If there are broken teeth
  • To correct the disproportionate shortness of the teeth.

How is Aesthetic Filling made in Turkey?

Before the aesthetic filling applications, the operation area is anesthetized with the local anesthesia method. Tooth decay or teeth in the part to be filled are cleaned. Subsequently, the cavity filling material is shaped for better adhesion. In addition, beveling (a process that increases the surface area to which the filling will adhere) is performed to ensure better adhesion of the filling materials to the teeth. In this way, the filling and tooth tissue cling stronger and become much more durable. By making the necessary corrections on the surface of the tooth enamel, the filling material of the appropriate color and consistency is prepared and placed in the opened tooth cavity. In order for the filler to be at the desired hardness, it must be irradiated with halogen light each time. If the filling is too high or protruding, it is corrected until it is brought to the right level, and the process ends by polishing the teeth. The filling process is comfortable and quite comfortable for the patient. Factors such as the size and shape of the teeth, the interaction of teeth and gums, and the color of the gums should be evaluated while making aesthetic fillings for anterior teeth. In order to obtain a more aesthetic smile, a successful result can be obtained with bonding (adhesive) systems that can be applied to every part of the mouth. With a session duration of 1-2 hours, our patients have an aesthetic smile in a single session. The success of aesthetic fillings on anterior teeth is that the materials used can imitate dental tissues. If there is a color problem in the front teeth, the colored tissue is abraded and then aesthetic filling is applied according to the tooth colors of the patients.

Control after Aesthetic Filling process

Food dyes in tea, coffee, cigarettes and consumed foods can change the color of your filling along with your teeth. However, there is no difference between your natural teeth and your aesthetic filling. In some patients, the tooth may experience hot and cold sensitivity for 1-2 weeks until the body gets used to the filling. If the sensitivity does not go away, you should visit your dentist. If the fill height is high, there may be some sensitivity as it will be exposed to more pressure. When you notice such a situation, visit your dentist to remove the excess parts of the filling. After aesthetic filling procedures, you may feel a slight gingival pain, and your gingiva may be red. If the spaces between the teeth are filled or the size and position of the teeth are corrected, the pronunciation of some sounds may not be clear.

What to expect after Aesthetic Filling Treatment?

Among the advantages of aesthetic dental filling are:

  • Since aesthetic fillings are the same color as the tooth, it is very difficult to distinguish them when viewed from the outside.
  • It is many times longer than ordinary fillings.
  • They have a natural and aesthetic appearance
  • Relieves aesthetic concerns caused by black fillings
  • Composite dental filling is a procedure that can be performed on patients of all ages.
  • Aesthetic filling does not cause any allergic reaction.
  • Its surface is wear-resistant and functional
  • The process is simple and short in time
  • It is fully compatible with oral tissues, does not cause hot and cold sensitivity. has such benefits.

What are the advantages of Aesthetic Filling Treatment?

Aesthetic tooth filling application time is a subject that patients wonder. It is a fairly simple and fast procedure. The duration of this process varies according to the position of the tooth to be filled, the number of teeth to be restored, the extent of the damage to the tooth and the complexity of the work. Generally, a period of 30 to 60 minutes is required for each tooth. If it is necessary to restore 4 to 6 teeth, the procedure can take about 5 to 7 hours. Before the aesthetic filling process, you can talk to your dentist and get an estimate of the duration of the procedure.

Frequently Asked Questions about White Filling

How much are Aesthetic Filling (Composite Filling) cost in Istanbul?

Aesthetic filling prices are slightly higher when compared to gray amalgam dental fillings, which have a bad appearance. However, considering the aesthetic appearance, the price difference is definitely worth it. Composite dental filling prices vary according to the quality of the material used, the brand, the applications required for oral health, the size of the deformation in the teeth and the number of teeth to be filled. For example; Root canal treatment may be required due to caries before aesthetic tooth filling. In addition, the experience of the dentist also affects the prices of aesthetic fillings.

When can I return to daily life after Aesthetic Filling?

After this procedure, there is no harm in returning the patient to his daily routine. Care should be taken to follow only the warnings and instructions given by the dentist.

Does Aesthetic Filling hurt?

Before the aesthetic filling process is performed, local anesthesia is applied to the area to be treated and the area is numbed. During the filling process, the patient does not feel any pain or pain. Therefore, the patient does not experience any anxiety about suffering.
