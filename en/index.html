Dental Implants

A dental implant is an artificial tooth root made of titanium material, which is made to restore function and aesthetics in cases of tooth deficiency. Whether the tooth is extracted immediately or will be used replace a tooth that was extracted previously, with a simple procedure the dental implant is placed in the jawbone.

All on 4

All-on-4 dental implants are a natural-looking and more permanent alternative to dentures as well as a more comfortable solution. It is a full set of implants designed to replace your entire upper or lower set of teeth. Many patients who have dentures end up upgrading to All-on-Four because of the many added benefits this procedure offers.

All on 6

All on six procedure is generally undergone by restructuring a patient’s mouth when a dental patient has lost a significant number of teeth in one or both jaws. All-On-6 Dental Implants do not usually require bone grafting, meaning they are the perfect solution for patients with low bone density or volume in their jaw.

Zirconium Crown

It is a durable and biocompatible, natural tooth coating application. It positively affects the design of the smile. In the infrastructure of zirconium crown, white coloured material is used instead of metal material. For this reason, it is ideal for those with metal allergies. Also, it does not result in gray coloration like metal material.

Gum Treatment

Gum diseases occur as a result of bacterial infection. Most adults may experience gum problems at some stage in their lives. Non-surgical treatment is possible in the early stages. However, in some cases, surgical intervention may be required. For this reason, early diagnosis and treatment of gum disease are of great importance.

Hollywood Smile Design

Hollywood smile design is an aesthetic dental procedure and a special process for each patient in order to achieve the most natural and most beautiful appearance according to the face shape and needs. Crooked teeth can be straightened by making the root canal treatment because it avoids sensitivity due to extra shaving.
